RACCA

RACCA is an entrepreneurial venture founded on the 18th of July 2023
at the Tunku Abdul Rahman University of Management and Technology
library.Due to rapid internet advancement and government reinforcement
of safety precautions from COVID-19, people nowadays prefer to spend
most of their days at home working or scrolling endlessly on social
media. With this in mind, ambitious individuals with a wide professional
skill set came together as a team to create the ClickFood website.

More About Us

Location

RACCA headquarters is located at Tunku Abdul Rahman University of Management and Technology (TARUMT) Penang branch. Our team members optimise the use of facilities within campus such as the library to complete tasks on web development.

Members

Our organisation is composed of 5 core members, Allyne is the designer of our website, giving birth to the website logo, signature palette and overall layout. Rayden and Ee Wei are the main developers, who implement their efficiency and problem solving skills into coding the base of our website, Statistics and analysis are handled by Chloe who works fast with numbers and tables, helping us with advanced calculation, producing charts and solutions. Agus helps with further research towards pre-existing websites of related business nature to implement better features into our website.

Products

Our company also needs to provide a big variety of products for customers with reasonable pricing that is also very important for the company because who would rather pay less than pay more , this is humanity. Also our company will always be doing sales and promotions, because people are attracted to discounted items. Therefore, we can provide the utmost quality food and beverages from farm to table to every family.

Features

We ensure that users can navigate our website smoothly as we have a more user-friendly interface including graphic elements are easy to identify and understand, as well as helpful tooltips to guide them. The website includes an FAQ page to guide users on how to make the most of our website features and clear their doubts on anything they fail to comprehend on how it works. allows users to register for a membership that offers perks such as frequent buyer discounts through a points collection system.
